Gameplay

Built to kill you.

Every run is different. Every death is permanent. Norman Lives combines roguelike tension with deep RPG progression in a hostile procedural world.

⚔

Permadeath Survival

One life. No saves. No second chances. Death erases everything — your weapons, your levels, your progress. Start over and try again.

⚙

Procedural Worlds

Infinite terrain generated from seed. Dense forests, crumbling cities, scorching deserts, frozen tundra — no two runs explore the same map.

⚡

Deep Combat

14 weapons across melee and ranged. 6 damage types. Dodge rolls, quick-swaps, and weapon rarity from Common to Legendary.

♔

RPG Progression

Level up to unlock sprint, dodge roll, armor slots, HP regen, and more. Every level makes you stronger — until permadeath resets it all.

♕

Epic Boss Fights

Four multi-phase bosses guard the wasteland's deadliest zones. Alpha Mutant. War Machine. Hive Queen. Sentinel Core.

♖

Co-Op Multiplayer

Survive together with 2-4 players via Game Center. Share the wasteland. Share the danger. Permadeath still applies.
